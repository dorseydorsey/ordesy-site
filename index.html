Dorsey is a queer artist and musician connecting art and technology. They graduated from Calarts with a focus in pop production and experimental tape music. They currently work as a freelance producer, recording, mixing and mastering engineer. Their practice continues to study the strange and whimsical through the exploration of video and audio tape, signal processing, modular synthesis, and video synthesis.
